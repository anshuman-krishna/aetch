# Aftercare basics

A tattoo is an open wound for the first two weeks. Treat it like one.

## Day 1-3

- Keep the bandage on for as long as your artist tells you (often 2-24 hours).
- Wash gently with unscented soap and warm water, twice a day.
- Pat dry with a clean paper towel — no rubbing.
- Apply a thin layer of unscented moisturizer.

## Day 4-14

- Keep moisturizing 2-3 times daily.
- Don't pick or scratch as it scabs and peels.
- No swimming, soaking, or saunas.
- Keep it out of direct sun.

## Long-term

- SPF 30+ on the tattoo for life — sun is the #1 cause of fading.
- Re-moisturize regularly to keep the skin healthy.
- Touch-ups are normal, especially on hands, fingers, and feet.

If anything looks wrong — heavy redness past day 3, pus, fever — see a doctor. The AETCH Aftercare AI can help you triage common questions, but is not a replacement for medical care.
